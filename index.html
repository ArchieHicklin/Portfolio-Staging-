About me

I’m a 24 year old British designer and developer. For the last five years I have lived and worked in China, helping to solve design problems for early stage startups.

In 2012 I moved to Shenzhen, Asia's Silicon Valley, having made the tough decision to leave Edinburgh University whilst studying for my MA in Chinese to follow my passion for design.

Since then I have worked on a range of hardware, software and art projects across the world, building websites, apps, bots and design systems for startups in Asia, the US and Germany.

My design work is on display in over 20,000 stores in Mainland China, Hong Kong & Macau.
